Who We Are

About BV Being We

We're more than just an NGO — we're a community of compassion-driven individuals with a core team of 10+ passionate members and the unwavering support of 100+ volunteers across Vizag, Hyderabad & Bangalore.

We believe in the transformative power of community action. From educating girls to organising blood camps and distributing blankets in winter — we show up where it matters.

What We Do

Our Impact Areas

We provide hope through action — one initiative at a time.

Education Support

Supporting the education of underprivileged girls — covering school fees & providing stationery to unlock futures.

Blood Donation Camps

Partnering with Indian Red Cross, Mother's Blood Bank & Amma Blood Bank — fulfilling 7 out of 10 requests we receive.

Orphanage & Elder Care

Donating groceries, food & medicines to orphanages and old-age homes — bringing comfort where it's needed most.

Awareness Campaigns

Running rural campaigns on menstrual health, breast cancer awareness & sex education — knowledge is empowerment.

Environmental Action

Beach clean-ups and green drives to protect natural resources and promote sustainable living in our communities.

LGBTQ+ Advocacy

Collaborating with the LGBTQ+ community to champion equality, acceptance & inclusion for every individual.

Disaster Relief

Standing in solidarity during calamities — distributing 500+ blankets across rural & urban areas in peak winters.

Financial Assistance

Helping families who lost loved ones in accidents — providing a lifeline during their darkest hours.

Looking Ahead

Vision 2030

Our Motto: HOPE

By 2030 we envision the BeingWe Home — a sanctuary where orphaned children, elderly individuals and beloved animal companions can find refuge, care & love.

Through compassion, empathy and inclusivity, we aspire to create a community where every individual is valued, every voice is heard and every life is cherished.

“Join us on this journey of hope — one act of kindness, one gesture of compassion and one step closer to a brighter tomorrow.”
